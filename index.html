PDMS Traning & Support Services

We offer customer specific training in PDMS/E3D to suit individual company needs. We offer advanced training within all modules of PDMS. Our training solutions are not of the shelf but we always adapt the course content to suit our client requirements and the participant mix.
We offer advanced training in PDMS/E3D administration, PML2 & .net programming and customisation. We also offer follow-up trainings and online support for our training customers.
We can offer training at our training facility in Abudhabi / Trichy, or company location or online (For PML, .net courses only) 
Regular courses are always held on weekdays, online courses can be customised to suit client time zones.
All our courses are limited only to 4 participants as that help us to provide individual attention
